Last year the site was doing as many as 1500 tests a day during the peak of the pandemic. Then as the covid19 vaccine rolled out and more people were fully vaccinated demand for testing plummeted to under 100 tests a day in early June. The County responded by strategically reducing sites while still offering testing across the county. Easily meeting testing demand until now.
