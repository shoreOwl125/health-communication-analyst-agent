For a benign liver tumor, these are things which usually do not grow quickly and they do not leave the liver and colonize other areas of the body and cause harm. Cancer, on the other hand, can. Cancer invades the normal tissues, spreads throughout he liver and can go elsewhere in the body and ultimately, can unfortunately take a patient's life.
