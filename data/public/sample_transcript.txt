Today we are going to walk through five warning signs of diabetes that people often miss. 
First, frequent thirst and urination can happen when blood sugar levels remain high. 
Second, unexplained fatigue can be a sign that your body is not using glucose efficiently. 
Third, blurry vision can happen when fluid shifts affect the lens of the eye. 
Fourth, slow-healing cuts or frequent infections may be connected to high blood sugar. 
Fifth, unexplained weight changes can be a reason to speak with a healthcare professional. 

This video is educational and does not replace medical advice. If you notice these symptoms, consider writing them down and discussing them with a qualified clinician.
