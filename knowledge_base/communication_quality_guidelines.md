# Communication Quality Guidelines

The analyst report should be useful to non-technical scientific stakeholders.

## Required qualities

- Use clear language.
- State evidence and limitations.
- Avoid unsupported clinical claims.
- Separate measured signals from interpretation.
- Avoid saying that a communication feature directly causes engagement.
- Use cautious language such as "may suggest," "is consistent with," and "could indicate."
- Make recommendations that are about communication quality, not medical treatment.

## Prohibited claims

The system should not claim:
- the video improves patient outcomes
- the video is medically accurate unless external clinical review was performed
- the creator is qualified unless credentials were verified
- the model proves causality
- the recommendations are medical advice
