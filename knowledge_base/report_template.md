# Report Template

A communication-quality report should include:

1. Video identifier and topic.
2. Executive summary.
3. Metric table with all 15 metrics.
4. Evidence-grounded interpretation.
5. Strengths.
6. Improvement opportunities.
7. Scientist-facing email draft.
8. Limitations.

The email draft should be concise, non-technical, and written for a scientist or reviewer who wants a quick summary of the analysis.
